<div data-body-lang="pt">

<div data-body-lang="en" style="display:none">

I'm Felipe Tomé Pereira, a Data Analyst at Votorantim's Center of Excellence. My work involves applied research in machine learning — with a focus on graph neural networks (GNNs), anomaly detection, and ML pipelines for industrial data.

This blog comes from a simple need: documenting the journey. Over the past few years I've accumulated scattered notes, semi-documented experiments, and insights that lived only in memory. I want to change that.
